    A Little About Cat...

    Cat is a self-taught painter from Saint Louis, MO. While mainly focusing on solid color pieces, she also dabbles with various crafts and other art experiments. Each piece is carefully drawn out and positioned on staple-back canvases, followed by acrylics applied with a steady hand. While Cat loves to paint, she is also an aspiring Web Developer, who just happened to create this website from scratch! To learn more, click to follow at the top of the page (currently only using instagram), or contact her by e-mail for a commissioned piece!
